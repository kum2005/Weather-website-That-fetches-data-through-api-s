Fun Facts About Climate

1. The Earth Was Warmer 50 Million Years Ago

During the Eocene Epoch, about 50 million years ago, the Earth's climate was so warm that palm trees and crocodiles lived in the Arctic!

2. Cities Create Their Own Microclimates

Urban areas, with their concrete and asphalt, create "urban heat islands" where temperatures can be several degrees higher than surrounding rural areas.

3. Plants and Climate Change

Some plants can actually help reduce climate change! For example, mangroves sequester large amounts of carbon, helping to mitigate greenhouse gas emissions.

4. Sahara Desert Was Once Green

Around 10,000 years ago, the Sahara Desert was lush and green, with lakes and rivers. This period is known as the African Humid Period.

5. The Influence of Oceans

Oceans act as a climate buffer, absorbing about 90% of the heat from global warming, which has kept temperatures on land more stable but also caused ocean warming and rising sea levels.
