Information about the Winter Season

Winter in India usually begins in November and lasts until February, with January and December being the coldest months.
The season is characterized by low temperatures, humidity, clear skies, and variable winds. In the north, the weather is
cool from November to February, with temperatures ranging from 10°C to 15°C. In the south, temperatures are warmer, averaging
around 24°–25°C in Chennai. The Dras Valley in Kashmir is the coldest place in India, with a recorded temperature of -45°C
in 1908

Types of Health Issues during Winter

  • Joint pains
  • Common cold
  • Flu (influenza)
  • Ear infections
  • Sinusitis
  • Respiratory ailments
